Our Process

Introduction

We will begin by asking for a non-confidential overview of your project or program and will answer your questions about our firm.

Non-disclosure Agreement

If both parties decide to continue we will execute a one-way non-disclosure agreement to facilitate a more substantive discussion about your project or program.

Consulting Agreement

Pathway Biotechnology will then draft a proposed scope of work, and upon approval, will execute a consulting agreement which includes payment terms.
